About me

My name is Ida, and I was born in the early 90's. Ever since I was old enough to operate a computer, I have been fascinated with everything it can do. I've spent many hours in front of a computer monitor, either writing, playing games, researching all the things I've been interested in learning about, or designing and developing web sites. Until 2021, web development and design was merely a hobby I turned to every now and again, mainly in putting together a new written role playing concept that I could enjoy with my friends.

I spent parts of my twenties studying to become a teacher, and after I was finished I taught a few years before I had my first child. While on maternity leave, I thought about if I really wanted to be a teacher for the rest of my life, and I started looking into what else I could do with my life in case I decided against teaching. I discovered a part time online study programme for Front End Development, and it intrigued me immediately. I enrolled in March 2021, and am hopefully finished with my studies by March 2025.
